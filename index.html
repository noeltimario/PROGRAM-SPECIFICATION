eBlotter Functional Modules

Core capabilities related to the Client and Backend operations.

Module
Description

Citizen Complaint Interface

Enables Submission & Status Tracking via Chatbot.

Enables Complainants/Citizens to Submit Online Blotter/Complaint and subsequently Track Case Status via the Chatbot interface. (Directly from the Complainant/Citizen actor).

Case Management & Updates

Allows Staff to Record New Incident Data and Update Case Status/Details.

Allows Barangay Staff/Secretary to Record New Incident Data and Update Case Status/Details as the case progresses through the system.

Conciliation & Mediation

Function for Staff/Secretary to Schedule meetings.

A function available to Barangay Staff/Secretary to Schedule Conciliation/Mediation meetings related to open complaints.

Records Access & Search

Used by all internal actors to Search & View Blotter Records.

Used by all internal actors (Staff, Administrator, Captain) to Search & View Blotter Records for investigation, review, and reference.

User & Role Management

Dedicated to the System Administrator for access control.

Dedicated to the System Administrator to Manage User Accounts & Roles for all Barangay personnel, ensuring proper access control.

Decision Review & Approval

Enables Captain/Lupon to Review & Approve Case Decisions before final closure.

The final stage in the case flow, enabling the Barangay Captain/Lupon to Review & Approve Case Decisions before final closure.

AI Reporting & Insights

Functionality for the System Administrator to Generate Statistical Reports.

Functionality for the System Administrator to Generate Statistical Reports (incl. AI Insights) for trend analysis and strategic planning.

Security & Compliance

Enforces end-to-end data encryption (HTTPS and database encryption) and strict role-based access control.

Enforces end-to-end data encryption (HTTPS and database encryption) and strict role-based access control to ensure data privacy and adherence to government regulations.
